How to Make a:
Menger Sponge Cake

Makes: 1 Menger Sponge Cake

approx. duration: 30 - 40 years.
1 medium kitchen knife
1 food grade scalpel
1 handheld 40w fiber laser with <0.1mm beam radius
1 modified handheld microtome
an adequate amount of conventional explosives
corresponding amount of weapons grade uranium (mileage may vary)
1 rubber spatula

INGREDIENTS:
1 meter cube of sponge cake
6.25m^2 of 1/4 in fondant
0.9 m^3 of frosting (optional)

1. score a 9 squares on each face of the cube

2. remove the center section of each grid

3. repeat steps 1 and 2 on each smaller section

4. after repeating about 2 cuts, a medium kitchen knife will become too large. switch to the food grade scalpel.

5. after about 2 more cuts, switch to the fiber laser.

7. after about 2 more cuts, even the laser will become too big. switch to the microtome

8. once the individual molecules and atoms become visible and scale becomes meaningless, switch to the explosives to initiate a compression of your uranium, if you did it right you should have caused the atoms to split, further dividing the cake by three.

9. at this point, the traditional menger sponge stops dividing, but you are free to continue to split the quarks and simples to your liking.

10. cover completely with fondant and frost to your liking

11. serve!
